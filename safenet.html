01 — Introduction

What Is
ResQNow?

ResQNow is a conceptual emergency response system engineered to improve real-world safety outcomes through intelligent community coordination. Rather than replacing established emergency services, ResQNow proposes a complementary layer of rapid, proximity-based human response.

The framework balances three critical imperatives: community-based response, privacy protection, and responsible technology use. Every architectural decision prioritizes the dignity and safety of the individual.

🎯

Proximity-First

Prioritizing the nearest qualified responders for fastest intervention.

🔒

Privacy by Design

Zero-knowledge architecture with ephemeral data handling.

🤝

Human-Centered

Technology that augments human response, never replaces judgment.

"The fastest emergency response is often the one closest to you."
— ResQNow Design Philosophy

03 — Workflow

How It
Works

01

Trigger Activation

User activates via app with single-tap panic or voice-activated emergency. System immediately locks location and begins event logging.

02

Proximity Matching

Algorithm identifies and notifies the nearest verified responders within the defined radius, ranked by response time and capability.

03

Response Coordination

Responders receive secure route guidance and real-time updates. User receives responder ETA and identity confirmation.

04

Event Resolution

Once resolved, all ephemeral data is purged. User confirms closure, and system logs anonymized outcome metrics only.

04 — Privacy

Privacy
Architecture

🔐

Zero-Knowledge

No central storage of user identity or location history. All matching happens in-memory and is discarded immediately.

⏱️

Ephemeral Data

All event data auto-purges within 24 hours. Location trails exist only during active emergencies and are user-controlled.

👁️

Opt-In Visibility

Users choose their visibility radius. Responders only see what's necessary for the specific event — nothing more.

Data Principles

ResQNow operates on a strict minimum viable data philosophy. We collect only what's operationally essential and retain nothing beyond the immediate need.

All analytics are anonymized and aggregated. Individual event data is cryptographically isolated and automatically expired — no exceptions.

08 — Boundaries

System
Limitations

01

No Medical Diagnosis

ResQNow facilitates response — it does not provide medical advice or diagnosis. All medical decisions remain with qualified professionals.

02

Geographic Constraints

Response quality depends on responder density. Rural or low-coverage areas may experience delayed response times.

03

Technology Dependency

Network outages, low battery, or airplane mode will impair functionality. Users should maintain offline backup plans.

04

Not a Replacement

ResQNow supplements — never replaces — professional emergency services. Always contact 911 for life-threatening situations.
